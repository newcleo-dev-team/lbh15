================
Metallic Cadmium
================

The vapour pressure of liquid metallic cadmium, above its boiling point 1040K, follows the next relation :cite:`book12`:


.. math:: 
   P_{Cd}\degree = 10^{-\frac{5771}{T} + 13.78 - 1.0867 \log{T}}
   :label: p_cd
