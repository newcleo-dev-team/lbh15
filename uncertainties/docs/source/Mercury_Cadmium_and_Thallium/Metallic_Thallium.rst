=================
Metallic Thallium
=================

The vapour pressure of liquid metallic thallium, above its boiling point 1746K, follows the next relation :cite:`book12`:

.. math:: 
   P_{Tl}\degree = 10^{-\frac{9463}{T} + 13.364 - 0.892\log{T}}
   :label: p_tl
