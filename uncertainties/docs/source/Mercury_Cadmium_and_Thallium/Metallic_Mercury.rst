================
Metallic Mercury
================

The vapour pressure of liquid metallic mercury, above its boiling point 629.73K, follows the next relation :cite:`book12`:

.. math:: 
   \log{P_{Hg}\degree} = -\frac{3332.7}{T} + 12.6706 - 0.848\log{T}
   :label: p_hg
