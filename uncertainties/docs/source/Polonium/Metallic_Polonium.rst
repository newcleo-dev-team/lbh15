=================
Metallic Polonium
=================

The vapour pressure of liquid metallic polonium, from 641 to 877K, follows the next relation (Abakumov 1974a) :cite:`book2`:

.. math:: 
   P_{Po} \degree = 10^{-\frac{5440\pm60}{T}+9.46\pm0.05}
   :label: met_po
