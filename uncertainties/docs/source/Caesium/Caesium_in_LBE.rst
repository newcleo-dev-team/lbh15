==============
Caesium in LBE
==============

The  activity coefficients range from 0.001 at 723K to 0.013 at 1023K, indicating that – as expected – Caesium is retained in LBE :cite:`book20`:

.. math:: 
   \gamma_{Cs(LBE)} = 10^{-\frac{2677 \pm 863}{T} + 0.75 \pm 0.98}
   :label: gamma_cs_lbe
